LOTT SHIP AGENCY

A SHORT PRESENTATION

 

For more than four decades, William B. Lott, Jr. has been one on the most active actors on the Gulf Coast in support of the Offshore Oil Industry,

His company, Lott Ship Agency, has served expertly and reliably the extremely varied offshore logistical requirements of more than a dozen major operators in the Gulf of Mexico and beyond, both national and international, in particular Allseas, Saibos, Saipem, Subsea 7, BP, ExxonMobil, Noble Drilling, Aker Marine Contractors, Aker Solutions, DOF , BOA, GC RIEBER, Acergy, Gulf Offshore, or Sonsub.

The tasks performed by Lott Ship Agency's clients were as diverse as offshore drilling, offshore construction, heavy lift, or the very large Macondo Remediation operations related to the Deepwater Horizon oil spill of 2010, and also the extensive operations related to the salvage of BP and Exxon Mobil's Thunder Horse PDQ when it nearly capsized in Hurricane Dennis (2005).

Lott Ship Agency's operations have involved the simultaneous service of dozens of offshore construction ships, supply vessels, barges, crew ships, and helicopters, with the support of a direct shore crew exceeding at times fifty operators in locations all along the southern shores, from Brownsville to Miami.

The above-mentioned activities were always performed in full compliance with all extant State and Federal regulations, and in particular with all the provisions of the Jones Act.

In addition to himself Bill Lott's core managerial team includes Tyrone Kemper and Marc Moszkowski, both with extensive referrals and very long professional experience in the region and worldwide.
